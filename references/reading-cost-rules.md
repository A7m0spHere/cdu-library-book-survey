# Reading Cost Rules

Use reading cost to avoid recommending books that are correct but unusable for the user.

| Cost | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 低 | A student at the stated stage can read directly |
| 中 | Requires one or two related courses, but is suitable with effort |
| 高 | Research-level, graduate-level, heavy math/code/English, or too specialized |

## Common Examples

- Introductory/current-course textbooks: 低 to 中.
- Drug design after medicinal chemistry: 中.
- CADD and molecular simulation before structural biology: 中 to 高.
- Advanced molecular machine learning or English research monographs: 高.

Always explain why a high-cost book is still recommended, or move it to later-stage/external supplement.
