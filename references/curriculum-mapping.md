# Curriculum Mapping

Use curriculum mapping when the user asks what to read now versus later. If the exact CDU training plan is unavailable, mark the mapping as `[人工判断]`.

## Pharmacy/AI Drug Discovery Approximation

| Course/module | Typical role | Book types |
|---|---|---|
| General chemistry | Chemical foundation | General chemistry, analytical chemistry |
| Organic chemistry | Structure/reactivity foundation | Organic chemistry, medicinal organic chemistry |
| Biochemistry and molecular biology | Biomolecular foundation | Biochemistry, molecular biology, pharmaceutical molecular biology |
| Medicinal chemistry | SAR and drug classes | Medicinal chemistry, drug structure optimization |
| Pharmacology | Mechanisms and targets | Pharmacology, clinical pharmacology |
| Pharmaceutics/PK | Formulation and ADMET | Pharmaceutics, biopharmaceutics, pharmacokinetics |
| Drug design/CADD | Computational design | Drug design, CADD, molecular simulation |
| Chemoinformatics/bioinformatics | Data and modeling | Chemoinformatics, QSAR, bioinformatics |
| AI drug discovery | Modern application | AI drug design, ML, deep learning, RDKit |

## Report Wording

Use:

- `现在读`: matches current course/stage.
- `后续读`: requires one or two prerequisite courses.
- `考研前读`: central to exam review.
- `科研时读`: useful for projects or thesis work.
