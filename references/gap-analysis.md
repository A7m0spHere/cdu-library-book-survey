# Gap Analysis

Classify collection coverage by learning module.

| Coverage | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 已覆盖 | Recent or classic holdings are available and usable |
| 部分覆盖 | Some holdings exist but are old, non-borrowable, too general, or missing subtopics |
| 明显不足 | Few/no holdings for the module, or holdings do not support the user's goal |

## AI Drug Discovery Gap Modules

- Medicinal chemistry and SAR
- Pharmacology and target mechanism
- Drug design and CADD
- Chemoinformatics and QSAR
- Molecular docking and virtual screening
- RDKit and molecular data processing
- Machine learning/deep learning for molecules
- Bioinformatics/omics and target discovery
- PK/PD/ADMET modeling

## Recommendation

For each gap, state:

- what is covered by holdings
- what is missing
- whether to borrow, read in-library, buy externally, or suggest purchase to the library
